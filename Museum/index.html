TATTOO MACHINE

The first electric tattoo machine was patended by Samuel F. O'Reilly on December 8, 1891.

MODERN TATTOO METHOD

Depending on the tattoo machine speed settings, a tattoo needle will penetrate your skin between 50 and 3,000 times per minute. The needle is dipped in ink and the needles pierce the skin, making tiny holes for the ink to flow into. The movement of the ink into these tiny holes is called capillary action.
